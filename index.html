I was graduated for the bachelor of an economy. However, after a year's working and due to a whim of learning code in order to to be more productive on my job, I started to try to figure out what is programming. I was then impressed on how amazing coding is and felt like it is changing the world!

After struggling on current comfortable life or chasing what I really want to be, I quit the previous job and started my web developer learning career.

I learned HTML, CSS and vanilla Javascript, and React on online resource (udemey, youtube, etc..) and the tech books. Learning always comes with challenge and pain, especially the new world. However, all I could feel is exciting and the sense of accomplishment when I absorbed new knowledge or finished a project.
