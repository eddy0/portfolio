After struggling on current comfortable life or chasing what I really want to be, I quit the previous job and started my web developer learning career.

I learned HTML, CSS and valina Javascript, and React on online resource (udemey, youtube, etc..) and the tech books. Learning always comes with challenge and pain, especially the new world. However, all I could feel is exciting and the sense of accomplishment when I absorbed new knowledge or finished a project.
